What is an Equity?

Equity refers to the ownership interest in a company or property. It represents the value that remains in an asset after all debts and liabilities have been paid. In finance, equity is commonly used to refer to stocks or shares in a company, which represent ownership and entitlement to a share of its profits and assets.

Why Invest in Equity?

Investing in equity offers several benefits, including:

  • Potential for long-term growth.
  • Stable income through dividends.
  • Diversification of investment portfolio.

Equity investments help achieve financial goals such as saving for retirement or funding children's education.
